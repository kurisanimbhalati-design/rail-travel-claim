Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a trip?

Open the app, tap "Log New Trip", and fill in the date, route, distance, and purpose of your trip. Tap "Save" when you're done.

How do I export my claim to Excel?

Go to the Export screen, select the trips you want to include, and tap "Export to Excel". The app generates a completed RAL claim form that you can share via email, AirDrop, or save to Files.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your device's Settings, tap your Apple ID at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find RAL Travel Claim and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open the app and tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Can I use the app on iPad and Mac?

Yes. RAL Travel Claim works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Your subscription covers all your devices under the same Apple ID.

My data — is it safe?

All your trip data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
